Transferring Selected Items Between Devices

Instead of transferring entire categories between devices that are backed up onto a single MEEM cable, you can select individual items to transfer between devices, making it easy to share data between devices.

Always remember that you are transferring items from data stored on MEEM to the device that is currently plugged in.

Firstly, go to the Your data page where you will see all the devices backed up on the MEEM cable and select the icon of the device you want to transfer from. (A)

You will now see the selected device’s Your data page. Chose the category of the individual item(s) you want to transfer (B) and once in that category, press the Select button at the top of the screen and scroll through the contents choosing each item you want to share.

When you have selected everything you want in the category, press the Share icon (a box with an arrow pointing out to the top), which will bring up a prompt Add to Library. Press this prompt and all the selected items will be saved to the phone that the MEEM cable is currently plugged into.

Follow the same procedure for individual items in each category and the items will be saved to their respective folders on the device.
