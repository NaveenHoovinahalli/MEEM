100% Visible

With MEEM, all your backed up data is completely visible to you within the MEEM app. To see all your saved data go the main menu and into the Summary section. There you can see all the categories and the amount of information backed up within them. To see your saved items individually, tap on a category. This will allow you to see each item and individually or collectively send and share items via email, text, and social media. If you have any items saved in Archive mode, tap on the tab marked Archive in each category. For more information and detailed information on seeing all of your data in the Summary menu, please see the Summary section of the Help guide.
