Original Device Manual Backup and Restore

1. Device Backup

Drag icon (A) onto icon (B) on the right.

2. Device Restore

Drag icon (B) onto icon (A) on the left.

3. Category Backup

To back up an individual category drag the category from (A) to (B) ( left to right).

4. Category Restore

To restore and individual category, drag the category from (B) to (A) ( right to left).
