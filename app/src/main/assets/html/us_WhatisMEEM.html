WHAT IS MEEM?

MEEM is a mobile phone charger and backup device in one cable. So every time you charge your phone you back up the data stored on it to the MEEM cable itself.

Your memories are your business, safeguarding them is ours. We will never ask for your personal details or rights to your data for any reason. Your private information is always under your physical control.

No hacker abuse. No corporate exploitation.No government intrusion.
