Please follow the steps below to use the MEEM Desktop application function on your mobile device only after you have installed the application on your computer:

  • Connect the MEEM cable to the mobile phone and the Windows PC / laptop
  • Wait for the app to auto-launch on your smartphone or tablet
  • Stop the auto backup on the mobile device‚s app
  • Navigate to Menu > Modes of MEEM > select ”Desktop mode” and click ‚OK‚ in the MEEM pop-up (as shown below)
  • Once you select this mode, the cable will be disconnected from phone internally and the battery will not charge. If desired, you may disconnect the cable from the mobile device.
  • However, note that the cable should remain connected to the Windows PC / laptop

Wait for the MEEM desktop app to auto-launch in Windows PC / laptop Enter the 4 digit PIN, the one that was used to register / set up the new cable and click the ’Login’ button (as shown in the picture below).

After successful login, the default cable name ’MEEM’ will be displayed in the left hand pane of the app screen as shown in picture 14.
